What is NaughtyDog?

Founded in 1984, NaughtyDog has emerged as a prominent game development studio in the industry. Since its inception, the studio has dedicated itself to crafting immersive gaming experiences that captivate players worldwide. With a diverse portfolio spanning over a decade, NaughtyDog has brought to life an array of memorable titles across various genres. Among its notable releases are "The Last of Us" an epic RPG adventure set in a richly detailed fantasy world, and "Uncharted" a space exploration game that pushes the boundaries of player-driven narratives. Each game reflects the studio's commitment to innovation, storytelling, and pushing the boundaries of gameplay mechanics.

Our Mission

With a passionate team of developers, artists, and storytellers, NaughtyDog remains steadfast in its pursuit of excellence, striving to create games that resonate with players on a profound level. As we look to the future, we are excited to embark on new adventures, explore uncharted territories, and continue pushing the boundaries of what gaming can achieve.

Our Vision

At NaughtyDog, our vision is to inspire players through unforgettable gaming experiences that transport them to new worlds and ignite their imaginations. Our mission is to continue pushing the envelope of creativity, leveraging cutting-edge technology, and fostering a culture of collaboration to deliver gaming experiences that leave a lasting impact on players.
