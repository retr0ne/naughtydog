The Last Of Us Series

The Last of Us is an action-adventure-survival horror game series. The saga is set in a post-apocalyptic United States ravaged by cannibalistic creatures infected by a mutated fungus of the genus Cordyceps. It follows several survivors, including Joel, a smuggler who lost his daughter during the outbreak; Ellie, a young woman immune to the infection; and Abby, a Seattle soldier who becomes embroiled in a conflict.
