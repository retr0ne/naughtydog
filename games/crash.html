Crash Bandicoot Series

Crash Bandicoot is a linear platformer developed for PlayStation. Initially released in 1996, it is the first entry in the Crash Bandicoot series. This game begins with the protagonist of the series, Crash Bandicoot being experimented on by Doctor Neo Cortex, the main antagonist of the series and his assistant N. Brio to become the latest addition to Cortex's animal army created from enslaved animals across the Wumpa Islands.
