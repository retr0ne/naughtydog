Jak&Daxter Series

Jak and Daxter saga start with Jak and Daxter: The Precursor Legacy, was a very classic 3D platformer adventure that was originally release in 2001. With its sequel, the series veered into action with the inclusion of weapons and a darker tone. It also has some spin-offs, such as the racing title Jak X or Daxter, an installment starring Daxter, the co-star of the saga.
